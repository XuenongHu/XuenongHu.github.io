About Me

Hello! I am a Research Engineer at GE Vernova specializing in Sustainable Energy Systems. Based in Niskayuna, New York, my work focuses on advanced industrial R&D, accelerating innovation in grid modernization, and developing next-generation decarbonization technologies.

Prior to joining GE Vernova in 2026, I spent nearly 28 years at Shanghai Electric Group Co., Ltd., progressing from a hands-on power generation systems engineer to a Technical Lead and Project Manager. Over nearly three decades, I have spearheaded multi-million-dollar, large-scale sustainable engineering projects, led cross-functional teams, and deployed complex renewable integrations—helping bridge the gap between applied research and macro-level carbon reduction goals.

An alumnus of Tsinghua University (B.Eng.) and Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (M.Sc.), my core engineering philosophy is centered on utilizing data-driven simulation, thermodynamic optimization, and smart grid architectures to build stable, clean, and scalable energy infrastructure for the future.
