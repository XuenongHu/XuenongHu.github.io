About Me

Hello! I am a Research Engineer at GE Vernova specializing in Sustainable Energy Systems based in Niskayuna, New York. My core engineering philosophy centers on bridging the gap between cutting-edge theoretical simulation and practical, stable physical deployments.

Prior to my current role, I spent nearly 28 years with Shanghai Electric Group Co., Ltd., where I progressed from a foundational systems engineer to a Technical Lead and Project Manager. Throughout my career, I have specialized in transient stability modeling, green energy asset management, complex system reliability optimization, and executing multi-million-dollar energy infrastructure initiatives.

Here are a few core competencies and technical tools I focus on:

  • Renewable Integration
  • Smart Grid Infrastructure
  • MATLAB / Simulink
  • Python / Data Modeling
  • Energy Storage Systems
  • Thermal Power Optimization
